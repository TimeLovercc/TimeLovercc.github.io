Brief Bio

I am a graduate student at Penn State's College of Information Sciences and Technology (IST). I work on the challenges associated with reliable AI and controllable AI. For example, spurious correlation, knowledge editting and representation engineering.

  • 2021 - Present: Graduate student at Penn State. I work on reliable AI.
  • 2017 - 2021: B.Sc. at UESTC, advised by Prof. Lianli Gao and Prof. Linyuan Lv.
