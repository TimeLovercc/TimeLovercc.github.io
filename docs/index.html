Brief Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at Penn State's College of Information Sciences and Technology (IST). I work on the challenges associated with causal, robustness and graph. For example, spurious correlation, model explanation and out of distribution problems.

  • 2021 - Present: Ph.D. student at Penn State. I work on graph neural networks and causality.
  • 2017 - 2021: B.Sc. at UESTC, advised by Prof. Lianli Gao and Prof. Linyuan Lv.
