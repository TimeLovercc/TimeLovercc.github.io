Brief Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at Penn State. I work on the challenges associated with causal, robustness and graph. For example, counterfactual fairness, spurious correlation and out of distribution. I enjoy learning new things. Recently, I am interested in large language models and want to explore the direction of trustworthy for LLMs. I currently live in State College.

  • 2021 - Present: Ph.D. student at Penn State. I work on graph neural networks and causal inference.
  • 2017 - 2021: B.Sc. at UESTC, advised by Prof. Lianli Gao and Prof. Linyuan Lv.
